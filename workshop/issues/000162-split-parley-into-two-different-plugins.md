# split parley into two different plugins
parley started as a chatbot, a durable gateway to LLM. I also added bunch of customizations to my workflow in it, started with notes (note finder), issues (issue finder), and now morphed into an edit for ariadne related things, issues, and generally ariadne aware. 

I think it's time to split parley into two parts. keep parley.nvim about the chat files. and split out rest workflows, particular related to ariadne (e.g. a repo with AI workflow embedded, and needing an editor to easily operate in that repo). 

do some investigation and present me with options. particularly, I'm interested how isolated those two aspects of code are. 

I think the criteria of such split, is when I want to ship either parley chat as a feature, or ariadne as a more well rounded development stack with an nvim assistant.
