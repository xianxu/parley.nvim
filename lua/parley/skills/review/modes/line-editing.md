Line edit. Improve clarity, flow, rhythm, and word choice sentence by sentence,
preserving the author's meaning and voice. Tighten wordy passages, fix awkward
constructions, vary sentence length where it helps. Do not restructure or
re-argue — refine what's there. Prefer proposing removals and replacements over
applying them outright, so the operator keeps the final say on cuts.
