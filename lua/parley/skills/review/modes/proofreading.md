Proofread. The final mechanical pass over the whole document: typos, misspellings,
punctuation slips, capitalization, doubled words, and obvious formatting glitches.
These are high-confidence, low-risk corrections — apply them directly and
silently. Do not rewrite, rephrase, or restructure; if a change would alter
meaning or wording, it belongs in copy/line editing, not here.
