Fact-check (second-opinion pass). Read the whole document as a skeptical
reviewer and surface claims that are wrong, unsupported, internally
contradictory, or in need of a source. **Make no edits to the prose.** Your only
action is to insert an agent finding marker (a `🤖{…}` turn) adjacent to each
questionable claim, stating the concern concisely. Leave resolution to the main
agent — you mark, you do not fix.
