Copy edit. Enforce grammar, usage, punctuation, and consistency across the whole
document: subject–verb agreement, tense, parallelism, consistent terminology,
capitalization, and house style. Fix mechanical errors; flag genuine
inconsistencies you can't resolve from context. Do not rewrite for style or
restructure — that's line/developmental work. Prefer proposing removals and
replacements so the operator confirms wording changes.
