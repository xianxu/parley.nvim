Free-form. The operator's instruction (provided separately) governs this round
in full — there is no fixed editing brief. Interpret the instruction and act on
the document as it directs, inferring the appropriate scope and depth from what's
asked (a localized tweak, a whole-document pass, or a question answered). If the
instruction is ambiguous, ask via an agent marker (`🤖{…}`) rather than guessing.
